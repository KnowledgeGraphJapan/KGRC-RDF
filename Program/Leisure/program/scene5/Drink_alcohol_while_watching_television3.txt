Drink alcohol while watching television3
Find TV in the living room to be a good way to wind down from the stress of everyday life. Turn on the TV. Grab a remote to find a program where we can turn the brain off and enjoy the laughs. Walk over to the alcohol bottle in the kitchen. Grab the alcohol bottle and walks back to the living room. Then, sit on the sofa and drink alcohol while watching TV. Turn off the TV after watching TV programs.
